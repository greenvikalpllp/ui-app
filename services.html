Our Services

At Green Vikalp, we offer a range of comprehensive services designed to make biogas production efficient, sustainable, and accessible. Our expertise spans the entire process, from waste collection to energy generation, with a strong focus on promoting a circular economy. Here’s how we can support your renewable energy journey:

  • Biogas Plant Setup and Consultation : We specialize in designing and implementing biogas plants tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our experienced team guides you through every step of the setup process, including site assessment, plant design, construction, and commissioning. We ensure that each project is optimized for maximum efficiency and sustainability.

  • Technical Support and Maintenance : Ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of your biogas plant is a top priority. We offer ongoing technical support and regular maintenance services to keep your plant running at peak performance, minimizing downtime and maximizing energy output.

  • Training and Capacity Building : Empowering communities and businesses with knowledge is essential for the long-term success of biogas initiatives. We provide training programs and workshops on biogas plant operation, safety protocols, and sustainable practices, ensuring that stakeholders are well-equipped to manage and optimize their systems.

  • Consulting for Entrepreneurs : For those looking to start their own Bio CNG projects, Green Vikalp LLP offers expert consulting services. Our team, with extensive experience in setting up successful biogas operations, provides valuable insights and guidance. We help entrepreneurs with project feasibility analysis, financial planning, regulatory compliance, and technical know-how.
  • Waste-to-Energy Solutions : As part of our commitment to the circular economy, we convert degradable waste into Bio CNG, which can be used as a renewable fuel for various applications. We also offer solutions for utilizing by-products such as liquid and solid fertilizers, turning waste into valuable resources that contribute to environmental sustainability.

  • Sustainability and Impact Assessment : Partner with Green Vikalp to harness the power of biogas and contribute to a cleaner, greener future. Whether you're an industry leader, an entrepreneur, or a community looking to adopt renewable energy, our tailored solutions are designed to meet your needs and empower your sustainable journey.

  • End-to-End Biogas Solutions : From planning to operation, we provide a full suite of services across the entire biogas project lifecycle, including:
    • Waste collection and pre-treatment
    • Biogas production and storage
    • Energy generation and distribution
    • Post-production management and by-product utilization

    1. Feedstock Management for CBG Projects : Green Vikalp specializes in sourcing and managing feedstock for Compressed Biogas (CBG) plants to ensure consistent supply and operational efficiency. This includes:
      • Connecting farmers, vendors, and waste generators with project operators to minimize logistical challenges
      • Sourcing organic raw materials like agricultural residues, municipal solid waste, and food waste
      • Promoting waste-to-energy initiatives for environmental sustainability

    2. Fermented Organic Manure (FOM) Management : Green Vikalp supports the sustainable utilization of FOM, a by-product of CBG projects, by providing:
      • Market linkages with farmers, cooperatives, and agro-companies
      • Logistics support for efficient transport and distribution
      • Assistance with regulatory compliance and certification for marketability
      • Advocacy for circular economy practices by promoting organic manure over chemical fertilizers

    3. Government Incentives Assistance : Green Vikalp helps CBG project developers navigate and avail financial incentives offered by the Government of India and state governments. Their services include:
      • Awareness of schemes like SATAT, subsidies, and grants
      • Support with application preparation and submission
      • Ensuring compliance with regulatory guidelines
      • Liaising with government authorities for approvals and disbursal
      • Maximizing benefits from state-specific incentives such as land allotments and capital subsidies.

    4. Carbon Emissions Credit Services : Green Vikalp assists biogas and CBG project developers in earning carbon credits, contributing to both environmental and financial sustainability. This includes:
      • Assessing projects for eligibility under international carbon credit programs
      • Preparing documentation and data for carbon credit applications
      • Facilitating registration with accredited carbon credit agencies
      • Monitoring, reporting, and verifying emissions reductions
      • Connecting projects with buyers for trading carbon credits, generating additional revenue

      5. Green Vikalp’s comprehensive approach ensures the efficiency, sustainability, and financial viability of biogas and CBG projects.
