Welcome to Green Vikalp

At Green Vikalp, we are committed to providing comprehensive, sustainable solutions for biogas production and renewable energy generation. Our services are designed to support the entire lifecycle of biogas projects, from waste collection to energy generation and beyond. With a strong focus on promoting a circular economy, we aim to empower businesses, communities, and entrepreneurs to adopt green energy solutions that are efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally impactful.

  • Biogas Plant Setup and Consultation: Customized design, implementation, and consultation for efficient biogas plant setup.
  • Technical Support and Maintenance: Ongoing support and maintenance for optimal plant performance.
  • Training and Capacity Building: Providing training on operation, safety, and sustainable practices.
  • Consulting for Entrepreneurs: Expert guidance for launching Bio CNG projects, including feasibility and compliance.
  • Waste-to-Energy Solutions: Converting waste into Bio CNG and valuable by-products like fertilizers.
  • Sustainability and Impact Assessment: Tailored solutions for cleaner, greener energy adoption.
  • End-to-End Biogas Solutions: Comprehensive services across the biogas lifecycle, from collection to distribution.
  • Feedstock Management for CBG Projects: Sourcing and managing feedstock to ensure operational efficiency.
  • Fermented Organic Manure (FOM) Management: Supporting sustainable FOM use with market linkages and logistics.
  • Government Incentives Assistance: Helping developers navigate government incentives and ensure compliance.

From customized biogas plant setup to expert consulting, technical support, and government incentives assistance, our holistic approach ensures that every project we undertake is optimized for success. Whether you're looking to convert waste into valuable resources, develop a CBG plant, or access carbon credit opportunities, Green Vikalp is here to guide you at every step of your renewable energy journey. Our mission is to contribute to a cleaner, greener future, one project at a time.
